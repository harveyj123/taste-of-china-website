About Us

Taste of China offers delicious dining, takeout and delivery to Brooklyn, NY. Taste of China is a cornerstone in the Brooklyn community and has been recognized for its outstanding Chinese cuisine, excellent service and friendly staff. Our Chinese restaurant is known for its modern interpretation of classic dishes and its insistence on only using high quality fresh ingredients.
